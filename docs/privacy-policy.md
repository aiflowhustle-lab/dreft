# Dreft Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 15, 2026

Dreft is a local-first notes and canvas app for Mac and iPad. Your content stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

## Data we collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data.** Dreft has no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

## Data stored on your device

Dreft stores the following locally:

- Notes, canvas files, and bookmarks in your chosen vault folder
- App preferences (theme, sidebar width, open tabs)
- Security-scoped bookmarks so Dreft can reopen vault folders you select

## Photos and files

When you add images to a canvas, Dreft accesses photos or files **only when you explicitly select them**. Dreft does not scan your photo library in the background.

## Network use

Dreft works offline. The only optional network use is when you tap links in the app (for example, help or privacy pages) which open in your browser.

## Children

Dreft does not knowingly collect information from children.
