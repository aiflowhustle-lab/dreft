## Description

Dreft is a native SwiftUI workspace for thinkers, writers, and visual planners who want Obsidian-style power without giving up their data.

**Write in markdown**
Create linked notes with wikilinks, bookmarks, and a fast file explorer sidebar.

**Think on an infinite canvas**
Place note and image cards on a zoomable canvas. Connect ideas with styled lines, labels, and arrows.

**See the big picture**
Open the graph view to explore how your notes connect.

**Your vault, your device**
Everything is stored locally in folders you control. No account required. No cloud required.

**Built for Mac and iPad**
Custom chrome on Mac with split panes and keyboard shortcuts. Floating sidebar and touch toolbars on iPad.

**Export and restore**
Export canvases as PNG images. Browse version history and restore earlier snapshots.

Dreft respects your privacy: no tracking, no data collection, no ads.
